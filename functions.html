Key features

Local LLM

Utilizing the Ollama llama 3.2:1b, BMO has the ability to generate responses locally.

Text detection and text to speech

With OpenAI Faster Whisper and Piper TTS, BMO can hear and talk to you!

Lively expressions

BMO has a range of expressions to fit with the mood, have speaking fun with BMO!

Camera

With the Pi Ai Camera, not only can you take selfies, BMO will be watching you and make sure you are LOCKED IN.

Gaming

With ES-DE and RetroArch, you can go through a selection of Retrogames and play on the go!

Music

BMO can play some fire music for you with the Spotify Webplay API!
